2Timing Guys, LLC

2 Timing Guys was formed in 2012 as a partnership between Chris Clausen and Matthew Struckman to take over a popular race series, Dirt Crits, in St. Louis County that had existed for over a decade. A Jaguar Timing System was purchased from the series was expanded and we timed a few other events a year for other customers. In 2014 Chris moved to San Francisco and was bought out of the partnership by Matt.

Present day, 2 TG is a small family business run by Sally and Matt Struckman. 2TG is based out of Ballwin Missouri and has continued to grow every year. In 2012, Pfoodman Racing and 2TG combined with Valley Park to construct a 1.1 mile mountain bike trail on a piece of wooded property between the levee and the river. The team has continued to work on and improve the trail, as well as promoting several races in the continuing Dirt Crit Series yearly. Continually reinvesting in the company, our list of equipment and the size and number of events we can handle have grown. We currently promote and time almost 30 events per year, almost all events on trail or gravel.

As of 2015, 2TG has begun sponsoring the Pfoodman Racing Team. We are a group of about 25 men, women and children who have met through cycling since 2009, with Matt being one of the founding 4 members. Our team is family orientated, focused more on fun and camaraderie than results. We race in most cycling disciplines, with an emphasis on mountain biking and cyclocross and have raced all over the Midwest. The team has been a huge part of growing 2TG and help out at most 2TG events.
