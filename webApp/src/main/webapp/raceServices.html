Race Services

2TG is a timing and race promoter, with Matt having over a dozen years experience in promoting races. We can take your idea and create an entire event for you or we can simply time your event.

Services offered:

  • Race Director- Let us organize and control the race day stress
  • Timing Services-We utilize a Jaguar Timing System created here in St. Louis. No messy mats on the ground, our system hangs overhead on a truss that eliminates tripping hazards that can occur by mats and wires on the ground. We use disposable one time use chips, so there is no trying to collect chips from runners after the events. We can time bikes, runners, triathletes, remote control cars, kayaks, etc… If we can Velcro or stick a chip on it, we can most likely time it. Some of our chips will read at near 200 MPH!
  • We have all sorts of equipment to facilitate a race: stakes, cones, digital clocks, inflatable arch, pop up tents, etc… All of this can be used at your events. Sorry, but we typically do not rent out our equipment for an event we are not timing.
  • Preregistration- We can create and manage a pre-registration page for your event to help make the race day registration a little easier.



Call or email us today to discuss your event and get a price from us!
